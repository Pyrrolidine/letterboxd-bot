---------------
Commands
---------------

**!film/!movie/!f <film-name> (<year>)**

Search a film on Letterboxd. The year is optional and can also be specified with y: instead of parentheses.
__Example__: !film man knew too much y:1934
Returns the 1934 original Hitchock film, not specifying the year returns the more popular 50's film.

**!director/!d/!actor/!a <name>**

Search a filmmaker or an actor.

**!user/!u <username>**

Display informations and the featured favourite films of a Letterboxd member. It requires the Letterboxd username, not the display name.

**!review/!r <username> <film-name> (<year>)**

Display the reviews of a film from a specified user. The year is optional.
__Example__: !review porkepik floating weeds (1934)
Returns Porkepik's review of A Story of Floating Weeds (1934)

**!list/!l <username> <list-name>**

Display the list by the specified user.

**!checklb**

Check if letterboxd.com is down.

**!del**

Delete the last message the bot sent within a limit of the last 30 messages. The bot requires the \"manage messages\" permission.
