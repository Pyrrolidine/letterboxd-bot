!film/!movie/!f <film-name> (<year>)
Searches a film on Letterboxd. The year is optional.
!director/!d/!actor/!a <name>
Searches a filmmaker or an actor.
!user/!u <username>
Displays informations and the featured favourite films of a Letterboxd member.
!review/!r <username> <film-name> (<year>)
Displays the reviews of a film from a specified user. The year is optional.
!list/!l <username> <list-name>
Searches a list by the specified user.
!checklb
Checks if letterboxd.com is down.
!slowlb <seconds> (<timer>)
Enables a slow mode, optional timer is in minutes. Requires the manage messages permission.
!del
Deletes the last message the bot sent in a channel.
